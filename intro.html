Introduction: Cosmo Rhythm

Cosmo Rhythm consists of a series of ritualistic objects, with which I perform conscious repetitive actions. I use them as tools to experience the pure duration of time and given moment. I use different varieties of repetitive actions with the movement of my hands to create the patterns, such as rubbing, turning and dipping into liquid or dust. As a result the traces on the objects becoming traces of experienced ritualistic actions.
